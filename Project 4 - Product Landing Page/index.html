Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use this product?

For best results, use our hair product every time you wash your hair. Simply apply a small amount to towel-dried hair, style as usual, and enjoy a fresh, healthy hold that lasts all day.

Will this product make my hair look greasy or stiff?

Not at all! Our formula is specifically designed to provide a strong hold without any stiffness or greasy residue. It leaves your hair with a natural look and feel, while still being flexible and touchable.

Is this product suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our versatile formula works well on all hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly. It's designed to enhance your hair's natural texture without weighing it down or causing buildup.
